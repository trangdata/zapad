Do Not Feed

Natural meals are okay if they are caught by claw although hopefully I do not eat any poisoned rodents. Do Not Capture

My owners often come retrieve me and if I’m inside somewhere they cannot find me and become frustrated. Absolutely do not transport me by car without permission from my owners. If I am clearly and severely injured, please contact my owners; if you cannot get in touch, bring me to the address on my collar.
